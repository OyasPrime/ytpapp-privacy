# Privacy Policy — YT Playlist Sorter

**Last updated: May 10, 2026**
**Developer: OYas Prime**

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Google Account Information**: Name, email, profile picture for authentication only.
- **YouTube Data**: Your playlists are accessed for sorting. No data is stored on our servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate you via Google OAuth 2.0
- To read and reorder your YouTube Music playlists
- To manage your subscription status locally on your device

## 3. Data Storage

We do NOT operate servers or databases. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

## 4. YouTube API Services

This app uses YouTube Data API v3. By using this app you agree to:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [YouTube Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms)

Revoke access anytime: [Google Security Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
